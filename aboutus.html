About Us

P-Bay- now is innovation. The creator and designer of this e-commerce platform, P-Bay, is Vedant Shah who is currently in grade 10. Our company was established in June 2017 and we had decided in April, 2017 to go online and create P-Bay. It is a private online store meant for students at Northview Heights Secondary School and it helps students see different laptop brands and different prices side by side based on their affordability.

The Online Phoenix Store decided to sell laptops to Northview students because laptops nowadays are a necessity for students in high school as majority of their schoolwork is done online as they mostly would submit assignments and projects through sites such as Moodle, D2L, and Google Classroom. Also, laptops are a very prominent aspect of a high school student’s life because it enables students to connect with their friends through online social media and be able to work with their classmates on group projects through applications such as Skype. Moreover, everything relies on the online world and the laptops will assist the students in many ways. They will be able to research for their assignments as well as hand them in. Students would be able write assignments in applications such as Microsoft Word, and do much more with the laptops the Phoenix store intends to sell through the e-commerce platform P-Bay.

The Phoenix store has chosen to sell laptops to students at Northview Heights Secondary School via P-Bay because students would be able to buy laptops at a lower cost rather than spending a great amount of money for the same laptops from companies such as The Source or Best Buy. Another to why the Phoenix store will be selling laptops through P-Bay is because several students have only one computer or laptop at home and this can be hard for students to get schoolwork completed. Buying a laptop will allow them to finish their homework and assignments quickly as they would not have share one computer or laptop with their family members all the time. The Phoenix store intends to sell many types of laptops from many different companies that include Dell, Lenovo, HP (Hewlett-Packard), Sony, Asus, Acer, Microsoft, and Samsung. The list above also consists of Apple’s MacBook’s. The reason to why the Phoenix store has chosen to sell laptops from the companies listed above is because these companies are the best laptop brands of 2016 and 2017 and they want all students to get the best experience from their laptops.
